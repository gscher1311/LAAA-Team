Expected Buyer Objections & Our Responses

Objection: "The current return is too low."

Response: This is a value-add opportunity. The low current return is precisely why this property is an attractive investment. The path to market rents creates significant equity upon stabilization.

Objection: "The property is old and will require a lot of maintenance."

Response: We have factored in a conservative repairs and maintenance budget, as well as capital reserves, into our pro forma analysis. The age of the property is typical for the area, and the potential rental income more than compensates for the anticipated upkeep.

Objection: "Dealing with RSO and tenant turnover is a hassle."

Response: While RSO requires a strategic approach to management, it also ensures a stable tenant base. We can provide guidance on best practices for managing RSO properties and navigating the legal landscape to maximize your return.
